Rishton ka swad, Tulsi ke saath!

Tulsi tea is the household preference at the start of the day and frequently during the day too across Gujarat. Since 1981, its 9 delicious and richly satisfying variants and range of package sizes that cater to taste and need of different groups of people have contributed immensely to its popularity. Tulsi, a proud offering from G.M. TEA PACKERS PVT. LTD. has brewed success like nobody else – enjoying a prominent presence in rural to semi urban Gujarat through a vast network comprising 430+ distributors and 50,000+ outlets. Sales have been growing in double-digits year after year and reached substantial levels. Now the company is on its way to repeat the success story in MP and Rajasthan, taking forward the dream of Ghar Ghar Tulsi.
